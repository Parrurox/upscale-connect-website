Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11-December-2025

1. Introduction

UpscaleConnect (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you visit upscaleconnect.com and use our services.

By accessing or using the website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register for membership (name, email, business information, login details).
  • Complete your profile and membership directory information.
  • Participate in programs, events, mentoring sessions, and community forums.

We may also automatically collect Cookies and usage data (IP address, device data, pages visited, time on site).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize our services and member experience.
  • Manage your account and membership access.
  • Communicate with you about events, updates, and support.
  • Improve our website and offerings.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking tools to enhance your experience and analyze site usage. You may opt out of certain cookies through your browser settings.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We will not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the platform or if required by law or to protect our rights. Third-party analytics or social plugins may collect data through their own mechanisms.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We’ll post changes on this page with a new effective date.
